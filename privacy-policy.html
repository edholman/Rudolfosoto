Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 15, 2025  |  R Soto LLC | NMLS #1721163

R Soto LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit rudysoto.com and submit inquiries through our contact forms.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you voluntarily submit.
  • Mortgage Inquiry Information: Property address, loan amount, credit range, and other financial details relevant to a mortgage application.
  • Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and time spent on our site (collected via analytics tools).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your mortgage inquiries and provide personalized loan guidance.
  • To send you text messages and/or emails regarding mortgage services you have requested.
  • To comply with applicable federal and state lending regulations.
  • To improve our website and services.

3. Text Message (SMS) Communications

By submitting a contact form on this website, you consent to receive text messages from R Soto LLC regarding mortgage services, loan updates, and follow-up communications. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency may vary.

  • To opt out at any time, reply STOP to any text message.
  • To request help, reply HELP or contact us at the information below.
  • We do not sell or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

  • Lending partners and lenders for the purpose of processing your mortgage inquiry.
  • Service providers who assist us in operating our website or conducting business, subject to confidentiality agreements.
  • Regulatory authorities as required by law.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no internet transmission is completely secure.

6. Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies and third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics) to understand site usage. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To make such a request, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
